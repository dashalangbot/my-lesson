🎭 Student A

② Name a film that you think is overrated and one that is underrated. Why?

③ Has a film ever completely blown you away? Which one and what happened?

④ If you were nominated for an Oscar, what would your acceptance speech be about?
🎭 Student B
Listen, react, then answer yours:
① What film has made waves recently — in cinemas or on streaming?

② Have you ever felt that a film was too hyped — and then it didn't live up to the hype?

③ Who do you think was snubbed at any awards show you've seen? Was it fair?

④ Who would you want to host the Oscars — and why? Has anyone done a record-breaking job?
